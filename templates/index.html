    About the application

    Our application revolutionizes the way farmers detect and manage plant diseases. Powered by cutting-edge AI technology, it provides efficient and reliable disease detection, enabling farmers to take proactive measures and protect their crops effectively. With its user-friendly interface and accurate results, our application empowers farmers to make informed decisions and optimize their farming practices for higher yields and healthier plants. Say goodbye to guesswork and embrace the power of AI in disease detection and management with our groundbreaking application.

    Detect plant disease by leveraging power of Deep Learning

    Our deep learning model architecture is built upon the ResNet (Residual Neural Network) framework, known for its exceptional performance in image recognition tasks. By leveraging ResNet's skip connections and residual blocks, our model can effectively handle the challenges of training deep neural networks, leading to improved accuracy in plant disease detection. With an impressive accuracy of 0.98, our model demonstrates its ability to provide highly reliable and precise diagnoses, empowering farmers with the confidence to make informed decisions in disease management..

    Our plant disease detection process is seamless and user-friendly. Simply upload an image of the affected plant, and our AI-powered system swiftly analyzes it. Within moments, you'll receive comprehensive results and tailored recommendations for effective solutions to combat the identified disease, empowering you to take immediate action for better crop management.
